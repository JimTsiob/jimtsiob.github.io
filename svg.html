The above graph shows the fluctuation of Consumer Price Index (indicating inflation rises and decreases) throughout the years from 1967 up until February 2024.
Graphs like these are produced from Time-series analysis problems and provide analysts with some valuable insights on information that changes with time.
What I liked about it was that it can provide economists with useful information on how inflation will fluctuate in the upcoming monhts
and data scientists can also predict upcoming inflation percentages by feeding Time-series driven ML models with current data used in this visualization.
