Perception! Immersion! Empowerment! Superpowers as Inspiration for Visualization

In this paper the authors have created a study about using certain Superpower concepts which are used in comic books to
create better visualizations. The concepts are 7 and they are the following:

  • Enhanced vision: which is essentially a way for humans to see things from a different viewpoint than the casual eye can perceive.
  • Visual synesthesia: Make non-visual phenomena visual.
  • Enhanced attention: Identify details (no matter how important) in visualizations.
  • Enhanced numeracy: the ability to count visualized phenomena.
  • Enhanced recall: connect observations to prior ones or to other information.
  • Enhanced comparison: Find similarities/differences between things.
  • Enhanced prediction: Have the ability to make better predictions.

With all these in mind the authors suggest several ideas on how visualizations can be improved
providing readers with better insights and help them make better predictions.
What I liked about this paper is that the ideas are actually pretty solid, the authors mention the usage of computer vision
and several other tools to enhance visualizations based on the above concepts. Also, the paper's visualizations are very well drawn
in the form of comic book boxes, making the paper fun to read and stimulating the reader.
It's the only paper I've read with this style of visualization
